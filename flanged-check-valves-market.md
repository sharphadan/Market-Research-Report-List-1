<p><ul><li>Flanged Swing Check Valves</li><li>Flanged Ball Check Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flanged check valves market features primarily two types: flanged swing check valves and flanged ball check valves. Flanged swing check valves utilize a swinging disc to allow fluid flow in one direction while preventing backflow, making them suitable for larger pipelines. Flanged ball check valves, on the other hand, use a ball mechanism to achieve similar functionality but are often found in smaller applications. Both types are crucial in various industries, providing reliability and efficiency in fluid management systems.</p></p>
