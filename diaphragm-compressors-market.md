<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm compressors are categorized into three main types based on the number of stages: single stage, two stage, and multi stage. </p><p>Single stage compressors compress gas in one cycle, offering a simple design and lower cost but limited pressure capabilities. </p><p>Two stage compressors use two cylinders for improved efficiency, achieving higher pressures with better cooling. </p><p>Multi stage compressors have multiple compression stages, allowing for the highest pressures and greater versatility, suitable for demanding applications across various industries.</p></p>
