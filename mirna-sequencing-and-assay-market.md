<p><p>miRNA sequencing and assay markets are categorized by different sequencing technologies. Nanopore sequencing provides real-time, single-molecule analysis by measuring ionic changes as DNA passes through a nanopore, enabling long-read capabilities. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing detects pH changes associated with nucleotide incorporation, providing rapid and cost-effective sequencing. Each technology offers unique advantages, catering to diverse research needs in miRNA analysis.</p></p>
