<p><p>The parts washer market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water/aqueous-based types. Solvent-based washers utilize organic solvents to dissolve grease and oils, offering rapid cleaning and effective degreasing, but may pose environmental and health risks. In contrast, water/aqueous-based washers employ biodegradable detergents mixed with water, making them a safer and more eco-friendly option. While solvent-based models excel in heavy-duty applications, aqueous systems are ideal for routine maintenance and operations, highlighting their respective strengths in various industrial settings.</p></p>
