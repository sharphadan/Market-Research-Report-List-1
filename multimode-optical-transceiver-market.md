<p><p>The multimode optical transceiver market includes various form factors catering to different data rates and applications. CFP, CFP2, and CFP4 provide high-speed solutions for long-distance transmission. XFP supports 10 Gbps applications, and CXP is designed for ultra-high-speed data centers. Each type addresses specific networking needs, balancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
