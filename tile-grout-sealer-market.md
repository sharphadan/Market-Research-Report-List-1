<p><p>The Tile and Grout Sealer market comprises primarily Teflon-based and nano-based products. Teflon-based sealers utilize polytetrafluoroethylene, providing excellent water and stain resistance, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. They create a protective layer that repels contaminants. Nano-based sealers, on the other hand, utilize advanced nanotechnology to penetrate surfaces at a microscopic level, enhancing durability and providing long-lasting protection against moisture and stains. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity and appearance of tiled surfaces in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
