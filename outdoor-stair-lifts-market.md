<p><p>The outdoor stair lift market primarily comprises two types: straight and curved stair lifts. Straight stair lifts are designed for straight staircases, offering a more straightforward installation and typically lower costs. In contrast, curved stair lifts are tailored for staircases with bends or landings, requiring custom tracks and a more complex installation process, which often makes them more expensive. Both types enhance accessibility, providing a smooth and safe transition between outdoor levels for individuals with mobility challenges.</p></p>
