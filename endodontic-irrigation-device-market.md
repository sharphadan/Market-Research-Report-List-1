<p><p>The Endodontic Irrigation Device market is segmented into two main types: Ultrasonic Endodontic Irrigation Devices and Manual Endodontic Irrigation Devices. Ultrasonic devices utilize high-frequency sound waves to enhance the efficacy of irrigants, improving disinfection and debris removal during root canal treatments. In contrast, manual devices rely on traditional syringes and hand-operated tools to deliver irrigants, offering a more straightforward approach. Both types cater to specific practitioner needs, influencing preferences in endodontic procedures based on efficiency, control, and cost.</p></p>
