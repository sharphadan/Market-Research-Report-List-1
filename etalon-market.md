<p><strong>The Etalon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Etalon</li><li>Air Spaced Etalon</li><li>Piezo Tunable Etalon</li><li>Gires–Tournois Etalon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Etalon market types include Solid Etalons, which utilize a single solid substrate for precise optical interference; Air Spaced Etalons, featuring air gaps between substrates to achieve tunable wavelengths; Piezo Tunable Etalons, using piezoelectric elements for rapid wavelength adjustments; and Gires–Tournois Etalons, designed for high finesse and narrow linewidths, often used in laser systems. Each type serves distinct applications in optics and photonics, catering to industries such as telecommunications, spectroscopy, and sensing technologies, enhancing precision and performance.</p></p>
