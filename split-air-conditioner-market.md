<p><ul><li>Constant Frequency</li><li>Variable Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The split air conditioner market is categorized into two main types: constant frequency (CF) and variable frequency (VF) systems. Constant frequency models operate at a fixed speed, maintaining set temperatures by frequently cycling on and off, leading to potential energy wastage and temperature fluctuations. In contrast, variable frequency systems adjust compressor speeds based on cooling demands, providing more efficient temperature control and energy savings. VF units tend to be quieter and more environmentally friendly, catering to a growing demand for efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
