<p><strong>The Instant Coffee Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dry Coffee</li><li>Freeze Dry Coffee</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instant coffee powder market primarily comprises two types: spray-dry coffee and freeze-dry coffee. Spray-dry coffee involves rapidly drying liquid coffee through hot air, resulting in fine granules that dissolve quickly. This method is cost-effective and widely used. In contrast, freeze-dry coffee involves freezing brewed coffee and then removing moisture under vacuum, preserving flavor and aroma but at a higher cost. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with freeze-dry coffee often perceived as having superior quality.</p></p>
