<p><ul><li>2-Component Net Radiometer</li><li>4-Component Net Radiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The net radiometer market encompasses two primary types: the 2-component net radiometer and the 4-component net radiometer. The 2-component net radiometer measures incoming and outgoing shortwave and longwave radiation, making it suitable for basic energy balance studies. In contrast, the 4-component net radiometer provides a more comprehensive analysis by measuring both shortwave and longwave radiation in both upward and downward directions. This advanced model is ideal for complex environmental research and climate monitoring applications, offering enhanced accuracy and insight.</p></p>
