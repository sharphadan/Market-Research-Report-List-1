<p><strong>The Wafer Level Packaging Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging</li><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer Level Packaging (WLP) is an advanced packaging technology that offers compact and efficient solutions for semiconductor devices. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging involves placing the IC directly onto the substrate, with bonding pads on the die edge connected to the substrate for signal transmission. In contrast, Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging expands the die area to accommodate additional input/output connections, enhancing performance and power efficiency. Both types cater to high-demand applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and communication sectors, driving market growth.</p></p>
