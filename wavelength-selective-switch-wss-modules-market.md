<p><p>Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) modules are critical components in optical networks, enabling efficient signal routing based on wavelength. LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) based WSS modules utilize liquid crystal technology to control light paths, offering high performance with low insertion loss and broad wavelength range. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based WSS modules utilize micro-mechanical mirrors for precise light manipulation, providing compact designs and fast switching capabilities. Both types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications and data centers, driving advancements in optical performance.</p></p>
