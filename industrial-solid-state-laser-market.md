<p><ul><li>Pulse Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial solid-state laser market is primarily divided into two types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers emit energy in short bursts, providing high peak power, which is ideal for applications like metal cutting and precision machining. Continuous type lasers produce a steady beam of light, suitable for processes such as engraving and welding, where consistent energy delivery is required. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to diverse industrial applications and enhancing productivity and precision in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
