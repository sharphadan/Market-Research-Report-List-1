<p><strong>The Solid-State LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phase Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid-State LiDAR market features three primary technology types: </p><p>1. **MEMS Based Scanning** uses Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems to achieve precise angular scanning, offering compactness and improved durability. </p><p>2. **Phase Array** technology employs an array of sensors to electronically steer light beams, enabling rapid and efficient data capture without moving parts. </p><p>3. **Non-Scanning Flash** emits a full-field laser pulse to gauge distances simultaneously, resulting in quick and comprehensive 3D mapping, ideal for dynamic environments. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
