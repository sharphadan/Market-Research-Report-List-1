<p><p>The metallic volute pumps market is primarily divided into two types: single stage and multistage volute pumps. Single stage volute pumps are designed for low to medium flow applications, efficient for situations requiring moderate pressure. In contrast, multistage volute pumps consist of multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressures and handle larger volumes, making them suitable for industrial and high-rise building applications. Both types are essential for various industries, ensuring efficient fluid transfer and management.</p></p>
