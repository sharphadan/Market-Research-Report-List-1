<p><p>The car audio subwoofers market is primarily divided into two types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers are equipped with a built-in amplifier, making installation easier and providing greater sound output. They are ideal for those seeking a compact solution with minimal setup. In contrast, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier for operation, allowing for greater customization and scalability in audio systems. This makes them popular among audiophiles who prioritize sound quality and flexibility.</p></p>
