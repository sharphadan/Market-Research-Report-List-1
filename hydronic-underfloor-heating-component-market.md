Manifolds serve as distribution points, connecting the heating pipes and regulating the flow of water for optimal temperature control. Sensors monitor floor temperature, while thermostats allow users to set and adjust heating levels, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. Together, these components create a cohesive system for effective underfloor heating.</p></p>
