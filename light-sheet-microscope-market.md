<p><p>The light sheet microscope market comprises several types, primarily including Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy (SPIM) and Dual-View SPIM (DiSPIM). SPIM uses a thin sheet of light to illuminate samples, minimizing photodamage and enabling rapid imaging of biological specimens. DiSPIM enhances this technique by employing two objectives for simultaneous observation, providing improved resolution and depth information. Other market types may include variations like light sheet fluorescence microscopy and specialized setups, catering to diverse research needs in life sciences and materials science.</p></p>
