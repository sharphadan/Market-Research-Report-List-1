<p><p>Marine navigation software is classified into two main types: on-premise and on-cloud. On-premise solutions are installed directly on users' devices or local servers, offering greater control over data and customized functionalities but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, on-cloud solutions operate through internet-based platforms, providing flexible access, automatic updates, and scalability. This model reduces hardware costs and allows users to access the software from various devices, facilitating easier collaboration and enhanced data sharing among maritime stakeholders.</p></p>
