<p><ul><li>Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR)</li><li>Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diabetic Retinopathy Treatment Market is categorized into two main types: Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR) and Non-Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (NPDR). PDR is characterized by the growth of new blood vessels, often leading to severe vision loss and requires aggressive treatments such as laser therapy or vitrectomy. NPDR, while less severe, involves changes in the retina that could worsen over time; it is typically managed with lifestyle changes and monitoring, though some cases may also require medication or laser treatments.</p></p>
