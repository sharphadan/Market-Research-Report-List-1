<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartz powder market is primarily segmented into two types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing quartz powder into specific shapes under high pressure, producing durable products with consistent dimensions, often used in electronics and ceramics. In contrast, casting molding entails pouring a liquid resin mixed with quartz powder into molds, allowing for intricate designs and smooth finishes, commonly utilized in construction materials and decorative items. Both methods cater to diverse industrial applications, driving demand in various sectors.</p></p>
