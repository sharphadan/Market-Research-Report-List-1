<p><p>Geosteering technology in the oil and gas sector encompasses various market types. Logging While Drilling (LWD) provides real-time data on subsurface formations, enhancing decision-making. Measurement While Drilling (MWD) offers critical information such as trajectory and formation properties during the drilling process. Rotary Steerable Systems (RSS) allow precise control of wellbore placement. Drive Systems ensure effective rotational forces for optimal drilling. 3D Seismic/Grid Models aid in visualizing subsurface structures, while "Others" includes emerging technologies and tools enhancing geosteering efficiency.</p></p>
