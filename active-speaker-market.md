<p><p>The active speaker market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired active speakers. Wireless active speakers utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology, offering convenience and portability, ideal for modern, mobile lifestyles. They often are equipped with built-in amplifiers and require no additional equipment, making setup easy. In contrast, wired active speakers rely on physical connections, delivering stable audio performance with minimal latency. They are often favored in professional settings, such as studios and live events, for their reliable sound quality and robust performance.</p></p>
