<p><p>Infant milk formula is categorized into three main types: Starting milk formula, Follow-on milk formula, and Toddler's milk formula. Starting milk formula is designed for newborns up to six months, providing essential nutrients for growth. Follow-on milk formula caters to infants aged six months to one year, supporting their evolving dietary needs as solids are introduced. Toddler's milk formula is formulated for children aged one to three years, offering balanced nutrition to enhance development during early childhood. Each type fulfills specific age-related nutritional requirements.</p></p>
