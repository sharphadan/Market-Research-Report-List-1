<p><p>The outdoor temperature sensor market includes two main types: wired and wireless sensors. Wired outdoor temperature sensors are directly connected to a network or monitoring system, providing reliable data transmission but often requiring more installation work. In contrast, wireless outdoor temperature sensors use radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology to transmit temperature readings, offering greater flexibility and easier installation. Both types are essential for applications like weather monitoring, agricultural management, and HVAC systems, catering to diverse user needs and environments.</p></p>
