<p><strong>The Prefabricated Building Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton System</li><li>Panel System</li><li>Cellular System</li><li>Combined System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prefabricated building systems are categorized into four main types. The Skeleton System features a framework of columns and beams, allowing flexible interior layouts. The Panel System utilizes pre-made wall and floor panels, offering quick assembly and reduced labor costs. The Cellular System comprises modular units that create self-contained spaces, ideal for efficiency. Lastly, the Combined System integrates elements of all three, providing versatility and customization. Together, these systems improve construction speed, reduce waste, and enhance design flexibility.</p></p>
