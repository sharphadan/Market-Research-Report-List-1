<p><p>The commercial water filters market comprises several types, including Reverse Osmosis (RO) water filters, Ultrafiltration (UF) membrane water filters, and others. RO filters use a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities, ensuring highly purified water, making them ideal for sensitive applications like laboratories and food processing. UF membrane filters operate at higher pressure, effectively removing larger particles and microorganisms while retaining beneficial minerals. Other types encompass various filtration solutions, such as activated carbon filters and ceramic filters, catering to diverse commercial needs.</p></p>
