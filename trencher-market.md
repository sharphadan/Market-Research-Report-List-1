<p><ul><li>WheelTrencher</li><li>ChainTrencher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The trencher market is primarily categorized into two types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers utilize rotating wheels with sharp blades to cut through soil, making them ideal for soft to moderately hard ground. They offer higher speed and efficiency for shallow trenches. In contrast, chain trenchers employ a continuous chain with sharp teeth, allowing for deeper and narrower trenches, suitable for tougher soil conditions. Both types are essential in construction, utility installation, and landscaping applications.</p></p>
