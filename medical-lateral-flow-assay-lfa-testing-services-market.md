<p><ul><li>Sandwich Assays</li><li>Competitive Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Lateral Flow Assay (LFA) Testing Services Market comprises two primary types: Sandwich Assays and Competitive Assays. Sandwich Assays involve the capture of target analytes between two antibodies, producing a measurable signal, ideal for detecting proteins or viruses. In contrast, Competitive Assays require the target analyte to compete with a labeled analogue, resulting in a decrease in signal proportional to the amount of analyte present. Both types are widely used for rapid diagnostics in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
