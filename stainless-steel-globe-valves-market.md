<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel globe valves market features two primary connection types: flanged and threaded. Flanged connection valves are designed for robust applications, providing a secure, leak-proof seal and are commonly used in high-pressure systems. They feature raised or flat faces and are bolted to piping flanges. In contrast, threaded connection valves are easier to install and are ideal for smaller pipes and lower-pressure applications, using threaded ends for quick assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for residential and commercial uses.</p></p>
