<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robot Chassis market encompasses three primary types: two-wheeled, four-wheeled, and tracked chassis. Two-wheeled chassis are typically more agile and suitable for indoor environments, enabling tight navigation. Four-wheeled chassis offer stability and are ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications, providing a balance between speed and maneuverability. Tracked chassis are designed for rugged terrains, delivering superior traction and grip, making them suitable for outdoor exploration and environments with obstacles. Each type serves unique operational needs in robotics.</p></p>
