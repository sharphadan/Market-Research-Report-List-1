<p><p>The Low Pressure Injection Molding Polyamide market comprises three main types: granular, sheet, and powder polyamides. Granular polyamides are widely used for producing complex shapes due to their excellent flow characteristics. Sheet polyamides are utilized in applications requiring large, flat components, offering durability and heat resistance. Powder polyamides, favored for their versatility, enable the production of intricate parts through techniques like selective laser sintering. Each type caters to different industrial needs, emphasizing performance and material properties.</p></p>
