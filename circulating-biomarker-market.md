<p><p>The circulating biomarker market includes various types that aid in disease diagnosis and monitoring. Circulating DNA, especially cell-free DNA, is analyzed for genetic mutations and tumor presence, providing insights into cancer progression. Circulating tumor cells (CTCs) are intact cells shed from tumors into the bloodstream, offering information about tumor behavior and treatment response. Other biomarkers may include exosomes and proteins, which can reveal disease status and aid in therapeutic decisions. Together, these components enhance personalized medicine approaches.</p></p>
