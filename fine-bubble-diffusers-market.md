<p><p>The fine bubble diffusers market includes various types such as disc diffusers, tube diffusers, plate diffusers, and dome diffusers. Disc diffusers feature a flat design that efficiently releases air bubbles into water. Tube diffusers have elongated shapes, providing a larger surface area for better oxygen transfer. Plate diffusers consist of flat plates that distribute air over a wide area. Dome diffusers utilize a dome-shaped structure that enhances aeration efficiency, making them suitable for applications in wastewater treatment and aquaculture.</p></p>
