<p><p>Xylenes are a group of aromatic hydrocarbons primarily consisting of ortho-xylene, meta-xylene, para-xylene, and mixed xylene, each having distinct applications. Ortho-xylene is used in the production of phthalic anhydride and solvents, while meta-xylene is utilized for manufacturing isophthalic acid. Para-xylene is critical for producing purified terephthalic acid (PTA) for polyester fibers and plastics. Mixed xylene, containing all three isomers, serves as a solvent and in the production of various chemical intermediates, driving demand across multiple industries.</p></p>
