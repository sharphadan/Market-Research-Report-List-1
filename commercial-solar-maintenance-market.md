<p><p>The commercial solar maintenance market comprises three key types: monitoring, preventative maintenance, and repairs. Preventative maintenance includes scheduled inspections and servicing to mitigate potential failures and extend system lifespan. Repairs focus on addressing any identified issues or malfunctions, restoring the system to full functionality.
