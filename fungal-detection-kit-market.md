<p><p>The Fungal Detection Kit market comprises various types based on detection methods. Kits using antigens or antibodies in a solid phase involve immobilizing these biological molecules to capture fungal components for diagnosis. Enzyme-labeled antigen or antibody kits utilize enzymes conjugated to antibodies for enhanced sensitivity, producing measurable signals upon fungal detection. Substrate-based kits involve specific substrates that react with enzymes present in the sample, generating a detectable product. Each type offers unique advantages in diagnosing fungal infections effectively.</p></p>
