<p><p>The carotid stent market encompasses various treatment modalities for carotid artery disease, including Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty (PTA), Carotid Artery Stenting (CAS), and Carotid Endarterectomy (CEA). PTA involves balloon dilation to restore artery patency, while CAS uses stents to prop open narrowed arteries, offering a less invasive option. CEA, a surgical procedure, involves removing plaque from the carotid artery. These approaches vary in terms of invasiveness, recovery time, and long-term outcomes, catering to diverse patient needs in vascular health.</p></p>
