<p><p>The conveyor and conveying equipment market comprises various types tailored for specific material handling needs. Roller conveyors utilize rotating cylindrical rollers to move goods, enhancing efficiency in warehousing. Belt conveyors transport items using a continuous belt, ideal for a variety of applications. Overhead conveyors save floor space by utilizing an overhead track system for lightweight items. Pallet conveyors are designed for moving heavy loads on pallets. Other types include bucket conveyors and screw conveyors, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
