<p><p>The needle-free devices market features three main types: spring-loaded jet injectors, battery-powered jet injectors, and gas-powered jet injectors. Spring-loaded jet injectors use mechanical force to deliver medication through the skin. Battery-powered jet injectors utilize electric power to generate the necessary pressure for injection, offering flexibility and precision. Gas-powered jet injectors rely on compressed gas to propel the medication, often delivering high volumes quickly. Each type improves patient comfort and adherence by eliminating traditional needles.</p></p>
