<p><p>A piezoelectric dental scaler is a dental instrument that utilizes ultrasonic vibration to remove plaque, tartar, and stains from teeth. Unlike traditional scalers, which rely on manual effort, piezoelectric scalers operate on the principle of piezoelectricity, where electrical energy is converted into mechanical energy, enabling precise and efficient scaling. This technology enhances patient comfort and minimizes damage to soft tissues, making it a preferred choice in modern dental practices.</p><p>The piezoelectric dental scaler market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
