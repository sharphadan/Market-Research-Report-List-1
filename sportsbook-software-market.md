<p><p>In sportsbook software, market types refer to the ways bets can be placed on events. </p><p>A Single market allows bettors to place a wager on one event, offering straightforward outcomes. Multiple markets enable bets on several events at once, often increasing potential payouts. System markets feature combinations of multiple bets, providing a safety net if some bets lose. Finally, Chain markets connect several bets in a sequence, requiring all to win for a payout but allowing for higher stakes and rewards.</p></p>
