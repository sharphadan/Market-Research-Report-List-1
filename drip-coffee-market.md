<p><p>The drip coffee market encompasses various types, each with unique preparation methods. Ice drip coffee is brewed slowly over ice, creating a refreshing taste. Turkish coffee involves finely ground beans simmered with water and sugar. Cold brew coffee steeps coarsely ground coffee in cold water for an extended period. Espresso is a concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee.</p></p>
