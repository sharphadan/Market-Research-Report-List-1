<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backup camera market features two primary types of image sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them suitable for high-end vehicles. In contrast, CMOS sensors are more power-efficient, cost-effective, and increasingly offer high image quality, making them popular in a wider range of automotive applications. Both technologies aim to enhance safety by providing clear visuals during reversing maneuvers.</p></p>
