<p><p>MEMS optical accelerometers are sensors that measure acceleration using micro-electromechanical systems technology. The market can be segmented into multi-axis and single-axis categories. Multi-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along multiple directions simultaneously, making them suitable for complex motion tracking in applications like robotics and automotive systems. In contrast, single-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in just one direction, often used for simpler applications, such as tilt sensing in consumer electronics. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
