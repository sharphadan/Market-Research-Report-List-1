<p><p>Automated conveyor sorting systems are essential for efficient logistics and distribution. Linear sortation systems involve a straight conveyor path where packages are directed to specific destinations using diverting mechanisms, ideal for high-speed sorting in warehouses. Loop sortation systems, on the other hand, utilize a continuous loop design that enables products to be sorted in a more flexible manner, allowing for accumulation and minimizing bottlenecks. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in handling diverse merchandise.</p></p>
