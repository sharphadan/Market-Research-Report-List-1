<p><p>The servo drivers market can be categorized into three main types: Pulse, Analog, and Network. Pulse servo drivers utilize digital signals to control motor movements with high precision, ideal for applications requiring rapid response times. Analog servo drivers operate using continuous voltage levels, offering smooth control for simpler tasks. Network servo drivers integrate into communication networks, allowing multiple devices to interact seamlessly, enabling advanced automation and remote control capabilities. Each type caters to different application needs and performance criteria in automation systems.</p></p>
