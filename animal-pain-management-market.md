<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal pain management market is categorized into two primary types: external use and internal use products. External use products, such as topical analgesics and anti-inflammatory gels, are applied directly to the skin for localized pain relief and are often used for conditions like arthritis or injuries. Internal use products, including oral medications and injectables, are designed to address pain from within, targeting systemic issues or postoperative discomfort. Both categories aim to enhance animal welfare and improve quality of life.</p></p>
