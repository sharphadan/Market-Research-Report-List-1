<p><ul><li>Agarose Gel Electrophoresis System</li><li>Polyacrylamide Gel Electrophoresis System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal electrophoresis systems market primarily includes two types: agarose gel electrophoresis systems and polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis systems. Agarose gel electrophoresis is commonly used for separating nucleic acids, such as DNA and RNA, due to its ease of use and suitability for larger molecules. In contrast, polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis offers higher resolution and is ideal for smaller proteins and peptides separation. Both systems play vital roles in molecular biology, diagnostics, and protein research, catering to diverse scientific needs.</p></p>
