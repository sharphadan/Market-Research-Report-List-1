<p><ul><li>Manual Type</li><li>Motorized Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical damper market is primarily divided into two types: manual and motorized dampers. Manual dampers require physical adjustment to regulate airflow, providing simple operation suitable for smaller systems or applications needing infrequent changes. In contrast, motorized dampers utilize electric motors for automatic operation, allowing precise control of airflow and integration with building management systems. Motorized types are increasingly favored in larger installations due to their efficiency, convenience, and ability to enhance energy management in HVAC systems.</p></p>
